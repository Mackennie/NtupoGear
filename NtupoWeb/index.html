What is Ntupo Wear!

Ntupo Wear is a clothing company that was formed back in 2009 by Mbaki Selolwane with the hopes of selling kalanga classic wear, all the clothes branded kalanga words. The company wexpanded to sell more stuff like camp chairs, bottles and other merchandise still branded. Ntupo is owned by a young motswana and it has a goal of one day growing to hire other young people.
